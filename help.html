User Guide

How to Play:

Playing Wheel of Jeopardy is simple!

Enter player Names

Click

Take turns spinning The Wheel and responding to clues in order to score points

Play through two exciting rounds and challenge your friends

The player with the most points at the end of the second round wins!

To Customize Game:

All content on the Game Board is able to be edited. You can change Category, Clue and Answer.

Click

Simply click a square on the board to edit.

Popups will guide you through the process.

Clicking category names allow you to input new category names

Popups for dollar amounts allow you to change clues and answers associated with a given board location

An import/export feature also allows for exporting customized game boards Or importing saved boards

Addition of J!-Archive Import allows importing of entire games from The J! Archive site which is past games of Jeopardy! curated and maintained by fans.

When you have finished click

You’re now ready to play your customized game.
